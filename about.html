Meet the Founder

From Curiosity to Clarity

VerifAI began as a curious side project by a developer who noticed how easily identities were faked online. Frustrated by the increasing cases of digital impersonation and catfishing, he set out to build a tool that could verify identities in real-time. What started as a late-night experiment became a movement—a mission to make the internet a safer place. Today, VerifAI helps individuals and businesses detect fraud, expose impersonators, and build trust in a noisy digital world.

The journey wasn’t easy—building a face-tracking system that works across platforms, integrating bio comparison logic, and maintaining user trust required passion, persistence, and a few cups of midnight coffee. But the goal remains clear: to redefine digital safety, one face at a time.
